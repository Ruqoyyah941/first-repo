about us

Who we are

Nigeria's largest indigenous swamp rig services provider


We made a bold entry in the oil and gas upstream services via OES Energy Services - an integrated Oilfield Services Company. In demonstration of our high level of technical capabilities, the company is currently executing several oilfield services contracts in nigeria.


With the ain of being nigeria's largest indigenous oilfield services firm and encouraged by the local content drive in nigeria, we have invested over $400m in the acquisition of five oil drilling rigs for use in the niger delta.

vision

to be preferred integrated upstream oilfield services provider in africa creating value for all stakeholders.

strategy Statement

to be preferred upstream oilfield services provider in africa delivering quality, safe and efficient services by leveraging on the competency of our people, strategic partnership and technology with positive impact on our stakeholders.



core values


  • Accountability

  • Customer Satisfaction

  • Teamwork

  • Integrity

  • Originality

  • Nimbleness
Accountability

employees are expected to take responsibilities for decisions made, actions taken and the attendant consequences. Accountability is key to achieving Company’s business goals and overall success

Customer Satisfaction

The Company holds in high esteem the provision of exceptional service and innovative solutions to customers and clients. It is the responsibility of all employees to ensure products or services supplied /rendered by the Company meets or exceeds customer’s expectation.

Teamwork

Employees are required to work together as a team for the attainment of Company’s objectives by assisting fellow employees and stakeholders to meet their obligations. Employees are also required to maintain a professional work place relationship and make extra effort to ensure they create & engender a conducive work environment.

Integrity

  • Vendor Relations – our clients are important to us hence employees are required to treat every client with the same level of professionalism irrespective of the client’s image or name.
  • Conflict of Interest – Employees must avoid any activity that will conflict or be capable of conflicting with the Company’s business, interest and/or reputation.
  • Material benefits that might conflict/ interfere with proper performance of one’s responsibility must be declined or reported to the L&C department.

Originality

  • In OES, we do not believe in fitting in but rather our objective is to STAND OUT in all that we do so our image can showcase us.
  • To exhibit our originality, employees are expected to think and act autonomously by providing fresh and uncommon service to Company’s clients in line with guidance from Management.

Nimbleness

  • Employees are required to adapt to business climate changes with ease and strategic agility.
  • Employees are at all times expected to be proactive in reacting to change and embrace new challenges by doing things in best ways possible.
