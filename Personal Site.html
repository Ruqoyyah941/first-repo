Introduction

Home of Bradley is a page designed to teach and train
interested people selected vocational skills.
It is an innovative borne out of the curiosity to train
and empower people from the comfort of their homes
as a way to reduce the rate of unemployment and
keeping people engaged with any
of the vocational skills of their choice.

WHAT THIS SITE OFFERS?

Trainee will be:
  • Exposed to live trainings as well as dowloaded videos to enhance your training in your choice of course.
  • Able to download lessons of your choice for offline use
  • Able to comment and ask questions from the trainer(s) on your areas of difficulties
  • At the end of the training, trainee will be given projects to do on your own to access what you've learnt so far
  • Trainee will be awarded a downloadable certificate upon completion of your training and having certified all conditions of certificate claiming
